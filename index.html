Welcome to the NLP Group@OSU  

The research group's primary interests encompass Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Mining, and Artificial Intelligence (AI), with a specific focus on the critical role of language in both human and artificial intelligence. We're interested in applying NLP to various areas, including question answering systems, natural language interfaces, network analysis, and understanding human behavior. We have a strong interest in foundation models, such as large language models (LLMs) and multimodal models, as we believe they are key to advancing AI towards more general applications and we're dedicated to thoroughly studying them. Additionally, we prioritize factors like generalizability, interpretability, efficiency, and robustness in our research. Lastly, we're committed to translational research, leveraging AI to empower various domains like biology and biomedicine, and drawing inspiration from these fields to enhance AI development.
